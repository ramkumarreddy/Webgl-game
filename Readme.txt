Controls
---------
* T => Top View
* P => Player View
* C => Striker view
* Move the mouse to move the striker.
* Click the mouse to set the position of the striker.
* Click the left/right arrow keys to move the angle of firing of striker.
* Move the mouse away from the striker to increase the power of firing the striker.
* Click the mouse to fire the striker.

Rules
------
* Initial scores of players are 100 each.

* The score of the players reduces by 5 units for every 5 secs elapse of time.

* If player strikes coin of same color,then his score increases by 5 units.
			    other color,then his score decreases by 20 units.
